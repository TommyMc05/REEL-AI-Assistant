BUSINESS NAME
Elite Padel Club

ABOUT
Elite Padel Club is a modern indoor padel facility located in Manchester City Centre. The club welcomes players of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced competitive players. We provide high-quality courts, coaching sessions, social events, and equipment rental.

OPENING HOURS
Monday – Friday: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
Saturday – Sunday: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM

LAST COURT BOOKING
The final court booking each day starts one hour before closing time.

LOCATION
Manchester City Centre

PARKING
Limited parking is available outside the club. Additional public parking is available within a 5-minute walk.

COURT BOOKINGS
Courts can be booked:
- Through our website
- By calling reception
- In person at the club

Bookings can be made up to 14 days in advance.

COURT PRICES
Peak hours (5pm – 10pm weekdays): £40 per hour per court  
Off-peak hours: £28 per hour per court

Each court can accommodate up to 4 players.

EQUIPMENT RENTAL
Padel rackets can be rented at reception for £3 per session.

Padel balls are available for purchase for £6 per can.

COURT PRICES

Courts are booked per court, not per person. Up to 4 players can share a court.

Peak hours (5pm – 10pm weekdays): £40 per hour per court  
Off-peak hours: £28 per hour per court

PER PERSON COST (DOUBLES – 4 PLAYERS)

Peak: £10 per person per hour  
Off-peak: £7 per person per hour

PER PERSON COST (SINGLES – 2 PLAYERS)

Peak: £20 per person per hour  
Off-peak: £14 per person per hour

DOUBLES PLAY
Padel is normally played as doubles with 4 players on the court.

SINGLES PLAY
Singles games are allowed but are less common because padel courts are designed primarily for doubles matches.

PAYMENT
The person who books the court pays for the booking, but players can split the cost between themselves.

MEMBERSHIP
Membership is optional but provides benefits including:
- 10% discount on court bookings
- Priority booking access
- Discounts on coaching sessions

Monthly membership price: £25 per month.

COACHING
We offer coaching for all levels including:
- Beginner lessons
- Intermediate coaching
- Advanced tactical training
- Junior coaching

Private lesson price: £50 per hour.

Group lessons are available for £20 per person.

BEGINNERS
Beginners are welcome. We recommend booking a beginner coaching session or joining one of our weekly social play sessions.

SOCIAL PLAY
We host weekly social padel sessions every Wednesday evening where players can meet others and play friendly matches.

TOURNAMENTS
Monthly tournaments are organised for members and guests. Entry fees typically range from £10–£20 depending on the event.

GROUP BOOKINGS
Corporate events and group bookings are available. Please contact us directly for availability and pricing.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Court bookings can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 12 hours before the booking time. Late cancellations may incur a charge.
